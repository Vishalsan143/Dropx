Understanding Age Calculation: How Different Cultures Count Age?

Calculating a person's age may seem like a straightforward task, but it can vary widely depending on the culture and system used. At Dropx, our age calculator is based on the most common age system used in Western countries, where age increases on a person's birthday.

However, in some cultures, age is expressed differently, such as counting years with or without including the current year. For instance, in traditional Chinese age systems, a person is born at age 1 and increases their age at the Traditional Chinese New Year instead of their birthday.

It's important to note that calculating age can sometimes be confusing, especially when dealing with months and days. For example, our calculator considers February 20 to March 20 as one month, but there are two ways to calculate the age from February 28 to March 31. We use the former method, which counts February 28 to March 28 as one month and three days.
