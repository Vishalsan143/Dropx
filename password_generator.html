What is a Password Generator?
A password generator is a tool that automatically creates unique and complex passwords for you. It uses a random combination of letters, numbers, and symbols to create a password that is difficult for others to guess. With a password generator, you can create strong passwords that are not only secure but also easy to remember.
Why Do You Need a Password Generator?
Using a strong password is essential to protect your sensitive information from cyber threats such as hacking and identity theft. A weak password can be easily guessed or cracked, putting your personal and financial information at risk. Using the same password for multiple accounts also increases the risk of your information being compromised. A password generator ensures that your passwords are strong, unique, and not easily guessable.
